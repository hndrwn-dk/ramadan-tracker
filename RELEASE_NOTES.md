## Permissions Declaration (for Data Safety Form)

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `INTERNET` | Not used for data collection. Required by Flutter framework. |
| `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` | Calculate prayer times (Fajr/Maghrib) for Sahur/Iftar reminders. Location is stored locally only. |
| `ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION` | Fallback for prayer time calculation. |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | Display Sahur, Iftar, and goal reminder notifications. |
| `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` | Schedule precise notification delivery for prayer-time-based reminders. |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | Reschedule notifications after device restart. |

## Exact Alarm Policy Declaration

Required because the app uses `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`:

```
This app requires exact alarms to deliver time-sensitive Islamic prayer reminders 
(Sahur before Fajr, Iftar at Maghrib). These reminders must fire at precise times 
tied to astronomical prayer calculations, where even a few minutes delay would make 
the reminder useless (e.g., a Sahur reminder arriving after Fajr). The app also 
schedules goal check-in reminders at specific times throughout the day.
```
